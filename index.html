About Bam

A short bio that sells the voice quickly.

Brian Anthony Miller performs as Bam, a Fort Wayne-based stand-up comic with a dark, sharp, and highly observant style. His act mixes newsroom polish with open-mic grit, balancing dry commentary, local detail, and fast punchlines.

He is available for clubs, features, showcases, private events, and online collaborations.
