About

By day, Brian "Bam" Miller delivers the news on Fort Wayne's FOX 55 with a straight face and a sharp suit. By night, he's on stage delivering sharp punchlines while trying to keep a straight face.

A journalism grad from Columbia College Chicago and former improv actor, Bam built his comedy on the same instinct that makes him good at the news: find the story, then find what's funny about it — even when it's dark. Especially when it's dark.

He's been grinding the Fort Wayne open mic circuit — building a set that's confessional, self-deprecating, and just uncomfortable enough to be memorable.

Clean material available for private and corporate events.
